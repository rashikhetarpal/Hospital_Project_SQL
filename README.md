## ğŸ” Key Insights
- Appointment completion rate serves as a strong KPI for operational efficiency while cancellations and no-shows represent lost capacity and revenue opportunity.
- Revenue analysis shows that a relatively small set of doctors and treatments contribute disproportionately to total hospital revenue.
- Certain hospital branches outperform others despite similar appointment volumes, suggesting differences in patient demand or operational effectiveness across hospital locations.
